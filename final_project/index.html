A summary for newcomers

The Legend of Zelda is a sprawling video game series following the protagonist Link. This action-adventure/puzzle game typically casts Link as the hero on a quest to save the world, Hyrule, from an antagonist. While Ganondorf is the most well-known villain,However Link faces a variety of different evil forces throughout the series.
