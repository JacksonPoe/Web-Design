About me

Welcome to my website! Here, I'd like to introduce myself and share my passion for The Legend of Zelda series. My name is Jackson Poe, and at 18 years old, I'm currently a student at Chattanooga State Community College focusing on Computer Information Technology with a programming concentration. My current hobbies are swimming and hiking on trails.

My personal experience with zelda

Diving into The Legend of Zelda series holds a special place in my heart. It all began when I was just 4 or 5 years old, captivated by my very first video game: The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time for the Nintendo GameCube. The series instantly hooked me. The intricate puzzles challenged my young mind, while the captivating stories transported me to a fantasy world. It was a winning combination that ignited a lifelong passion for Zelda.

Why did you decide to make this website

This is a question I've been asked: why create a website about The Legend of Zelda? The answer is simple: my lifelong passion for the series. Ever since I was first introduced, Zelda has captivated me. I've grown up alongside it, and the respect I hold for it is immense. It might sound silly, but I truly believe the series has helped shape who I am today. Now, I want to recreate that same spark of passion in others. The Legend of Zelda has spoken volumes to me, and I want to share its magic by raising awareness for this incredible series.
