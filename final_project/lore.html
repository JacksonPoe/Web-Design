Zelda timeline

so this page is about the lore of zelda it gets complicated so im going to give you the timeline Before the Split: This period lays the groundwork for the entire series. We see the creation of the world by the golden goddesses, Din, Nayru, and Farore. They leave behind the Triforce, a powerful artifact granting wishes based on its holder's heart. Early games like Skyward Sword take place here, introducing Link, Zelda, and the constant struggle between good and evil personified by Ganondorf.

The child timeline

This new timeline emerges due to Link's warning. It depicts a Hyrule where Ganondorf's rise to power is prevented. Here, we find games like Majora's Mask, where a new villain menaces a parallel world, and Twilight Princess, where a dark and twisted version of Hyrule emerges due to a different sequence of events.

The adult timeline

Remember how Link traveled back in time in Ocarina of Time? Well, that leaves an adult Link in the original timeline! This "Adult Timeline" showcases the consequences of Ganondorf's reign. Hyrule falls into ruin, and Link faces a corrupted world. Games like Wind Waker depict a flooded Hyrule where Link sets sail on a grand adventure, while Breath of the Wild presents a vast, desolate Hyrule that Link must rebuild.

The fallen hero Timeline

This is the most straightforward path. Link defeats Ganon in the original Legend of Zelda, but the evil Ganondorf keeps returning to plague Hyrule. This timeline includes games like Zelda II: The Adventure of Link and Link's Awakening. Here, Link becomes a recurring hero who vanquishes Ganondorf through the ages.
